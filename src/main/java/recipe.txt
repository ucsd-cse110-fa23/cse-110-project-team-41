

Apple Tomato Egg Salad


Ingredients:

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 medium apples, cored and diced
- 2 medium tomatoes, diced
- 2 cups lettuce, diced
- 4 large eggs, cooked and diced
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, combine olive oil, apples, tomatoes, lettuce, and eggs.
2. Mix together until the ingredients are evenly distributed.
3. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
4. Serve the salad chilled or at room temperature. Enjoy!

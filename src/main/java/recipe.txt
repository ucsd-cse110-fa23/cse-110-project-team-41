

Vegetarian Sesame Noodles

Ingredients:
-1 package of MBC noodles
-1 tablespoon sesame oil
-2 tablespoons soy sauce
-1 tablespoon rice vinegar
-1 tablespoon honey
-2 teaspoons minced ginger
-1 clove garlic, minced
-2 tablespoons sesame seeds
-1/4 cup chopped scallions
-Optional: 1/4 teaspoon chili flakes

Instructions:

1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook MBC noodles to package instructions.

2. Meanwhile, make sesame sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together sesame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, ginger, garlic, sesame seeds, scallions, and chili flakes.

3. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water until cooled.

4. Place cooled noodles in a large bowl and pour sesame sauce over the top.

5. Toss noodles to evenly coat in sauce.

6. Serve warm or chilled. Enjoy!

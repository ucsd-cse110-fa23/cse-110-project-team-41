Quesadilla Avocado Tacos:

Ingredients:

-2 large flour tortillas
-1/2 cup shredded cheese of choice
-1 ripe avocado, diced
-1 tablespoon olive oil
-Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.

2. Place one tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle with cheese.

3. Top with avocado, salt, and pepper.

4. Place the second tortilla over the top and press down lightly.

5. Drizzle the top tortilla with olive oil and allow to cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the bottom of the tortilla is golden and crisp.

6. Flip the quesadilla and allow the other side to cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden and crisp.

7. Cut into wedges and serve with your favorite taco toppings! Enjoy!

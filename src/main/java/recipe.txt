Blueberry French Toast:

Ingredients: 
- 2 slices of toast
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons of blueberries
- 2 tablespoons of grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons of milk

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.

2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the eggs, blueberries, cheese, and milk until it forms a thick consistency.

3. Dip each slice of toast into the egg mixture and place on a greased baking sheet.

4. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes or until lightly golden brown.

5. Serve warm with your favorite toppings. Enjoy!

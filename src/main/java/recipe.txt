

Bacon and Egg Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:
-Bacon
-Eggs
-Lettuce

Instructions:
1. Cook the bacon in a pan over medium heat until it's crispy. Remove from the heat and set aside.

2. In the same pan, cook the eggs in the bacon grease until they're cooked through to your desired doneness.

3. Take one lettuce leaf and place a slice of bacon and the cooked egg inside.

4. Repeat with remaining ingredients.

5. Serve immediately. Enjoy!

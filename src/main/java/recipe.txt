Shrimp and Tomato Parsley Stir-fry

Ingredients:
-1 pound shrimp
-1 tomato, diced
-2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
-3 tablespoons olive oil
-¼ teaspoon garlic powder 
-½ teaspoon oregano 
-Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

2. Add the shrimp and season with the garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the shrimp are cooked through.

3. Add the tomato and parsley to the skillet and stir to combine.

4. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until the tomato is softened.

5. Remove from heat and serve. Enjoy!

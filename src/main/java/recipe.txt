Stir Fry Bowl with Rice

Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked white rice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 ounces beef, thinly sliced
- 4 ounces pork, thinly sliced
- 1 head of lettuce, shredded
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 2 eggs, lightly beaten

Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.

2. Add the beef and pork and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned.

3. Add the lettuce and broccoli florets and cook for another 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.

4. Push the cooked meat and vegetables to one side of the pan and add the eggs. Cook for a few minutes, stirring, until scrambled.

5. Mix the eggs with the meat and vegetables.

6. Serve the stir fry over cooked white rice. Enjoy!

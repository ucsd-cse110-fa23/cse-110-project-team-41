

Quick and Easy Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients: 
-2 eggs 
-2 cups cooked white rice 
-2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

2. Crack the eggs into the skillet and scramble until cooked.

3. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.

4. Add the remaining oil to the skillet and add the cooked rice.

5. Stir the rice for a few minutes until heated through.

6. Return the eggs to the skillet and stir together with the rice.

7. Cook for a few minutes until the eggs and rice are well combined.

8. Serve and enjoy!

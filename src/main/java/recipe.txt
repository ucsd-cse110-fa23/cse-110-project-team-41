Chicken Tomato Toast Bake

Ingredients:

- 2 cups diced cooked chicken
- 2 cups diced ripe tomatoes
- 2 cups diced toasted bread pieces
- 1/4 cup Italian-style salad dressing
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

2. In a medium bowl, combine chicken, tomatoes, and toast pieces.

3. Add Italian-style salad dressing, oregano, salt, and pepper, and mix until everything is evenly coated.

4. Transfer chicken mixture to a 9X13" baking dish.

5. Bake uncovered for 30 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and tomatoes are tender.

6. Serve warm. Enjoy!

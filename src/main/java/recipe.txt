

MBC 뉴스 이덕영입니다 Salad

Ingredients:

MBC 뉴스 이덕영입니다 (chopped)

1 large tomato (chopped)

1/4 cup cucumber (chopped)

1/4 cup red onion (chopped)

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped MBC 뉴스 이덕영입니다, tomato, cucumber, and red onion.

2. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar on top.

3. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

4. Toss everything together until all the ingredients are evenly coated.

5. Serve and enjoy!

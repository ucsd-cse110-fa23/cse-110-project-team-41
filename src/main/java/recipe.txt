

Chicken and Beef Omelette

Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts, cooked or grilled and chopped into cubes
- 1/4 lb ground beef
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1/4 cup milk
- 2 tomatoes, finely diced
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until thoroughly cooked. Drain any excess fat.
2. Add the chicken cubes to the skillet and cook until heated through.
3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk.
4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and stir to combine with the beef and chicken.
5. Add the diced tomatoes and season with salt and pepper.
6. Cook over medium heat until the omelette is cooked throughout, about 3 to 4 minutes.
7. Serve warm and enjoy!

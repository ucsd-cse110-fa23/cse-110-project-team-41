

Tortilla Pizza

Ingredients:

-1 tortilla
-2 tablespoons pizza sauce
-1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

2. Place tortilla on a baking sheet.

3. Spread the pizza sauce over the top of the tortilla.

4. Sprinkle cheese over the top.

5. Bake in preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.

6. Enjoy!

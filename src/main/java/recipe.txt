

Bacon and Chicken Frittata

Ingredients

- 8 slices of bacon, diced
- 1 cup of cooked chicken, diced
- 8 eggs, lightly beaten
- Salt and pepper

Instructions

- Heat a large skillet over a medium-high heat and add in the bacon.
- Cook the bacon until it is slightly crisp and brown, then add in the chicken and stir to combine.
- Once the chicken has been heated through, add in the beaten eggs and stir to combine.
- Cook the mixture, stirring occasionally, until the eggs have set and are just cooked through.
- Season with salt and pepper and serve warm.

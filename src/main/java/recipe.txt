Chicken and Tomato Toast

Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts
- 3 tomatoes, sliced
- 4 slices of toast

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. 

2. Place chicken breasts in baking dish.

3. Top chicken with tomato slices.

4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.

5. While chicken is baking, toast the slices of bread in a toaster.

6. When chicken is finished cooking, assemble the toast with slices of chicken and tomatoes. 

7. Serve warm.

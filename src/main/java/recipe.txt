Shrimp Tacos with Avocado and Salt:

Ingredients:

- 1 lb. Shrimp, shelled and deveined
- 4-6 Tacos
- 1 Avocado, cubed
- Salt, to taste

Instructions:

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the shrimp.
2. Cook the shrimp for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until cooked through.
3. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
4. Place the tacos in the skillet and cook until lightly browned and heated through.
5. Divide the shrimp among the tacos.
6. Add the cubed avocado and sprinkle with salt.
7. Serve the tacos and enjoy!

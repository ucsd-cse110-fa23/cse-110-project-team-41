Oatmeal Peanut Butter Banana Parfait

Ingredients:

- 2 cups old-fashioned oats
- 2 cups Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2 bananas, sliced

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix together the oats and Greek yogurt until combined.

2. Divide the mixture into two bowls and top each bowl with one tablespoon of peanut butter.

3. Slice the bananas and divide them evenly between the two bowls.

4. Layer each bowl with oats, yogurt, peanut butter, and bananas repeating the order of ingredients until you reach the top of the bowl. 

5. Enjoy your breakfast parfait!

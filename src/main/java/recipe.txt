Mushroom and Broccoli Beef Fried Rice with Crispy Chicken 

Ingredients: 

- 2 large chicken breasts, diced
- 2 eggs, lightly beaten
- 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
- 2 cups broccoli, diced
- 1/2 lb. beef, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 3 cups cooked white rice
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 2 green onions, diced

Instructions:

1. Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add diced chicken and cook until browned, stirring occasionally. When chicken is cooked through, transfer to a plate and set aside.

2. Heat remaining tablespoon vegetable oil in the same skillet. Add eggs and scramble until just cooked. Transfer to the plate with the chicken.

3. Add mushrooms, broccoli, beef, and garlic to the same skillet and cook until vegetables are lightly browned and beef is cooked through, stirring occasionally.

4. Add cooked white rice and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until vegetables are tender.

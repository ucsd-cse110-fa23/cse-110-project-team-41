Swordfish Basildon:

Ingredients:

- 1 lb of swordfish, sliced into 1-inch thick fillets
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- 3 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons of basil leaves, finely chopped
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 425Â°F.

2. In a large bowl, mix together the swordfish, olive oil, garlic, basil, and lemon juice.

3. Mix together until all of the ingredients are evenly coated.

4. Place the swordfish fillets in an oven-safe dish and season with salt and pepper.

5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.

6. Serve with a side of Basildon or your favorite sides. Enjoy!

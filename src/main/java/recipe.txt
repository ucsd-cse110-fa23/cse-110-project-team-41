Chicken Bacon Fried Rice:

Ingredients:

- 2 strips of bacon, diced
- 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, diced
- 4 eggs
- 2 cups cooked rice
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until it is browned and crisp. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside.

2. Add the diced chicken to the bacon fat and cook over medium heat for about 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Then add the cooked bacon back in.

3. Beat the eggs together in a small bowl and pour them into the skillet with the chicken and bacon. Cook, stirring constantly, until the eggs are scrambled.

4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and mix together with the chicken and eggs. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

5. Cook for an additional 5 minutes to heat the rice through. Serve hot.

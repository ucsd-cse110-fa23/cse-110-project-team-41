Butter-Steak and Roasted Potato Broccolidor

Ingredients:

- 2 steaks of your choice
- 2 potatoes
- 2 cups broccolidor
- 2 tbsp butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

2. Peel and cube potatoes, and place on a baking sheet with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper.

3. Roast in the oven for 25-30 minutes until potatoes are golden and crunchy.

4. In the meantime, heat a skillet on medium-high heat.

5. Add butter to the skillet, and allow to melt.

6. Once the butter is melted, add steaks to the pan.

7. Season with salt and pepper, and cook for about 4 minutes on each side, or until the steak is cooked to your preference.

8. In the same pan, on medium heat, add the broccolidor and saut√© for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

9. When the potatoes and steaks are done cooking, assemble the plates and serve. Enjoy!

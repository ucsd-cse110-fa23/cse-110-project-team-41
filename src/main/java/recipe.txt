Bacon, Egg, and Flour Wrap:

Ingredients:

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
2-3 strips of bacon, chopped
- 2 eggs, lightly beaten
- 1/2 cup cooked wheat
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat a greased skillet over medium heat.

2. Add the bacon, cook until it's crispy.

3. In a large bowl, combine the flour, eggs and wheat. Mix until smooth.

4. Form the mixture into small patties and cook them in the bacon grease.

5. Flip the patties over once they start to turn golden brown.

6. Once cooked, season with salt and pepper.

7. Serve warm with your favorite condiments. Enjoy!

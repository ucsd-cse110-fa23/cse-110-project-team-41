

Beep Salad

Ingredients:

-Beep 

Instructions

1. Separate the Beep into bite-sized pieces.

2. In a large bowl, mix the Beep with some olive oil, salt, and pepper to taste.

3. Add some chopped vegetables, such as tomatoes, carrots, bell peppers, and onions, and mix well.

4. Add diced cheese, olives, or other desired ingredients and mix.

5. Serve the Beep Salad with a slice of crusty bread. Enjoy!

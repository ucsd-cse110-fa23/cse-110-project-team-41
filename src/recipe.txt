

Fried Rice with Scrambled Eggs and Chicken

Ingredients:

- 2 large chicken breasts
- 2 cups cooked rice
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Instructions:

1. Cut the chicken into bite sized pieces and season with salt and pepper.

2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until golden brown and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

3. In the same skillet, add the onion and garlic and cook until the onion is softened, about 4-5 minutes.

4. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce and stir until evenly combined.

5. Add the chicken back to the skillet and stir everything together. Cook for another 2 minutes.

6. In a small bowl, beat the eggs. Pour the eggs over the rice and chicken mixture and turn the heat to the lowest setting. Cover the skillet and cook for 10 minutes.

7. Uncover the skillet and stir the egg and rice mixture together until the eggs are fully cooked

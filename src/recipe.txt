

Bacon and Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:
2 strips of bacon
1 chicken breast, cut into strips
2 tablespoons of grease

Instructions:
1. Heat the grease in a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the bacon strips and cook until crisp, about 5 minutes.
3. Add the chicken strips and cook for about 5 minutes or until chicken is cooked through. 
4. Once chicken is cooked, stir to combine with the bacon. 
5. Serve while still hot. Enjoy!

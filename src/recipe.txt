

Chili Cheese Brownie Bake

Ingredients: 

- 1 pound ground beef
- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
- 1/4 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons of salt
- 1 box of brownie mix

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.

2. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the ground beef is cooked through, drain off any fat.

3. Place the cooked ground beef in the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch baking dish.

4. Stir in the cooked chicken and spread it out evenly.

5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk and eggs.

6. Pour the egg mixture over the beef and chicken in the baking dish.

7. Sprinkle on the salt.

8. Prepare the brownie mix according to the directions on the box.

9. Pour the brownie batter over the beef and chicken in the baking dish.

10. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 30 minutes or until the top of the brownie is set and a tooth
